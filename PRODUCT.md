# Product

## Register

product

## Users

A single analyst monitoring East African music releases from a desktop or mobile browser. The user needs to add releases quickly, keep long-running view histories, compare momentum, and judge milestone likelihood without maintaining a backend.

## Product Purpose

Provide a dependable local YouTube view-velocity workstation. Success means the velocity and probability math is auditable, every observation survives browser restarts, polling resumes automatically, and high-density monitoring remains fast with long histories.

## Brand Personality

Precise, compact, operational. The interface should feel calm under heavy data and communicate in direct analytical language.

## Anti-references

Not a marketing site, SaaS dashboard, social-media manager, or decorative analytics demo. Avoid light cards, inflated spacing, gradients, oversized radii, ornamental icons, glass effects, and generic blue-purple product styling.

## Design Principles

1. Correctness before decoration: calculations and stored observations are the product.
2. Show the evidence: expose windows, projections, raw observations, confidence, and anomaly flags.
3. Optimize repeated monitoring: keyboard access, compact controls, and predictable navigation.
4. Degrade explicitly: every failure reports what happened and offers a recovery path.
5. Keep ownership local: credentials, history, notes, settings, import, and export remain in the browser.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG AA contrast for normal text, preserve visible keyboard focus, use labels in addition to color for status, support 375px through wide desktop layouts, and remove nonessential transitions when reduced motion is requested.
